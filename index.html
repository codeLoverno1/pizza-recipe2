Pizza

Ingredients

For the topping:

  1. 3/4 cup tomato paste
  2. 1/4 cup sundried tomatoes
  3. 1/2 cup mozzarela cheese
  4. 1/2 cup monterey jackcheese
  5. 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  6. 1/4 cup capers
  7. 1/4 cup pitted black olives, sliced
  8. 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  9. 2 tomatoes, sliced
  10. 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  11. 3 pcs meatballs, diced

For the dough:

  1. 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour, sifted
  2. 1 tsp salt
  3. 1 cup warm water
  4. 2 tbsp sugar
  5. 2 packages dry yeast
  6. 1/2 cup olive oil
